## Football Loans

- Available to borrowing clubs located anywhere globally, provided receivables originate from European clubs.
- Funding timeline: 4–12 weeks depending on loan type (transfer factoring ≈ 4 weeks; financial restructuring ≈ 12 weeks).
- Interest rate: 4%–6.5% per year.
- Broker fee: 0.75%–1.25% of the loan.
- Currency matches the home country.
- Maximum loan size: EUR 500,000,000.
- Borrowers can access 100% of guarantee value.
- Guarantees are backed by club revenue sources: TV rights, sponsorships, ticketing, and outstanding player transfer payments.
- Player transfer loans specifically are secured by transfer agreement instalment payments instead of traditional revenue guarantees.
- Loan terms extend up to a decade.

## Advertising & Sponsorship

- Runs international auctions for these placements (naming rights, jersey rights, even single-match deals), typically without a reserve price.

## The 77 Auctions

- Competitive, no-reserve bidding on advertising placements (e.g. front-of-jersey space).
- Example structure: bids accepted until a set deadline, but the club may close early with any bidder.
- Buyer cost: 12% of the bid, plus 18% VAT. No premium on the opening bid — premium applies only to the difference on later bids.
- Example listing: a 4th-division Brasília club offering ~600K TV audience/season and ~400K live spectators/season.
- Enquiries go to Dr Carl Peralta via WhatsApp.

## Audit findings this redesign concept addresses

- Left-sidebar nav overlapped its own logo divider at shorter viewport heights ("About" struck through the underline rule).
- WhatsApp offered as the only real contact path — many visitors are hesitant to hand over a personal WhatsApp number just to ask a question.
- Pages felt congested: dense repeated navigation, little breathing room between sections.

Fixes applied in the concept: responsive top nav (full link list preserved in the footer sitemap), a chatbot as the primary contact channel grounded in this knowledge base (WhatsApp/phone offered as secondary human handoff), and decongested section layout with clear grouping.
