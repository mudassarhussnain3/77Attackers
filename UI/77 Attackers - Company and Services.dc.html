Our story

Dr Carl Peralta graduated as a Lawyer (Advocate) in Malta (EU). In 2005 he founded '77 Great Estates' – a successful estate agency in Malta. After '77 Great Estates' brokered a substantial amount of property deals in Malta and overseas running into millions and tens of millions of Euro, the Group opted to branch out into their favourite sports: Football. Dr Peralta enjoyed tremendously playing football at local Club and University level in Malta and overseas. Therefore, in 2018, the Group combined their contacts in the local and international football market with their estate agency skills. They set-up '77 Attackers', a football agency which concentrates on a few but capable Attackers and Coaching staff.

Our mission

77 Attackers aim is to maximise the potential of each and every client while matching the ideal Attacker according to the requirements of the Football Club.
